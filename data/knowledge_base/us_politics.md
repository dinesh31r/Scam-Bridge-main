## Parliament (Sansad)

### Lok Sabha
- Source URL: https://sansad.in/ls
- Retrieved date: 2026-02-04
- Summary:
  - The Lok Sabha is the House of the People (lower house of Parliament).
  - Members are elected directly by the people under adult suffrage.
  - The maximum strength is 552 members.
  - Article 79 describes the composition of Parliament of India.

### Rajya Sabha
- Source URL: https://sansad.in/rs
- Retrieved date: 2026-02-04
- Summary:
  - The Rajya Sabha is the Council of States (upper house of Parliament).
  - It is composed of representatives of the States and Union Territories, plus members nominated by the President.
  - The Vice President of India serves as ex officio Chairman, and members choose a Deputy Chairman.
  - Article 79 describes the composition of Parliament of India.
