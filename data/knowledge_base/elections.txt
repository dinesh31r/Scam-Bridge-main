1) Election Commission of India (ECI) guidance
- Source URL: https://www.eci.gov.in/mythvsreality/
- Retrieved date: 2026-02-04
- Summary:
  - The Myth vs Reality Register is a repository of debunked election-related misinformation and aims to counter false narratives during election periods.
  - It is intended for public and media use to verify information and promote transparency and responsible communication.
  - The register is updated periodically as new claims are verified.
  - The ECI is a constitutional authority responsible for administering elections to the Lok Sabha, Rajya Sabha, State Legislative Assemblies, and the offices of President and Vice President.

2) Results portal privacy policy
- Source URL: https://results.eci.gov.in/privacy/index.html
- Retrieved date: 2026-02-04
- Summary:
  - The service states that, as a general rule, it does not collect personal information unless users provide it for a specific purpose.
  - Personally identifiable information volunteered on the application is not shared with third parties, and security measures are described.
  - Cookies are used for session, preference, and security purposes; users can refuse cookies with possible loss of functionality.
  - Data may be transferred to and processed in India, and the policy describes disclosure limits and security caveats.
